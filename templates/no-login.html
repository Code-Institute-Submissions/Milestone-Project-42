What's Mad Libs?

According to Wikipedia, Mad Libs is a phrasal template word game where one player prompts others for a list of words to substitute for blanks in a story before reading aloud

How it Works

Simply start to create a Msd Lib, pick a theme, add in words based on the words prompts (eg. noun, adjective, location etc.), and you'll generate a Mad Lib! Afterwards, in the View All page, you'll be able to edit or delete your Mad Lib

How do I get Started?

Simply Register yourself as a new user (if you don't have a user already), log in and you can start creating Mad Libs as well as view other entries by other users!
